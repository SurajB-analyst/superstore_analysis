# Regional Performance & Market Analysis Dashboard

## 1. Business Problem

Retail organizations operating across multiple regions require geographic visibility into sales performance, profitability, and market demand.

Without regional analytics:

* Underperforming markets remain unnoticed
* Growth opportunities are missed
* Resource allocation becomes inefficient
* Regional profitability varies without visibility

---

# 2. Project Objective

The objective of this project was to build a regional intelligence dashboard that helps management:

* Compare regional performance
* Identify profitable markets
* Monitor geographic sales distribution
* Detect underperforming regions
* Support territory planning decisions

---

# 3. Dataset Overview

The project used retail operational data containing:

* Regional sales records
* State-level performance
* Profit metrics
* Product categories
* Customer segments
* Shipping information

---

# 4. Tools & Technologies

* Microsoft Excel 2024
* Power Query
* Pivot Tables
* Maps & Regional Charts
* KPI Cards
* Slicers
* Conditional Formatting

---

# 5. Data Cleaning Process

* Standardized geographic fields
* Built state-region relationships
* Created regional helper columns
* Organized location-based analytical pivots
* Prepared performance comparison structures

---

# 6. KPI Definitions

### Regional Sales

Total sales generated by region.

### Regional Profit

Total profit contribution by region.

### Top Performing State

Highest revenue-generating state.

### Profitability by Region

Profit contribution relative to sales by region.

---

# 7. Dashboard Overview

The dashboard included:

* Regional KPI summaries
* State-level performance analysis
* Regional sales comparisons
* Geographic profitability visuals
* Top and bottom market analysis
* Interactive regional filtering

# 8. Key Insights

### Western region generated strongest sales performance.

### Certain states generated high sales but lower profitability.

### Regional profitability varied significantly.

### Some markets demonstrated strong growth potential.

### Geographic concentration affected overall business performance.

---

# 9. Root Cause Analysis

### Market demand differed significantly by geography.

### Regional pricing and operational costs impacted profitability.

### Product demand patterns varied across states.

### Resource allocation imbalance affected regional performance.

---

# 10. Business Recommendations

* Invest more heavily in profitable growth regions
* Improve underperforming market strategies
* Optimize state-level pricing and promotions
* Align inventory planning with regional demand
* Monitor regional profit contribution regularly
