# Superstore Business Analytics Portfolio

## Business Problem

Retail organizations generate large amounts of operational sales data across products, regions, customers, and discount strategies. However, management teams often struggle to convert raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence.

Key business challenges include:

* Identifying profitable and unprofitable product segments
* Understanding regional sales performance
* Monitoring the impact of discounting on profitability
* Detecting operational inefficiencies
* Supporting strategic decision-making with data

Without analytical reporting systems, retail management decisions become reactive instead of data-driven.

---

# Portfolio Objective

This portfolio was created to simulate real-world retail analytics use cases using Microsoft Excel.

The objective was to build multiple executive dashboards capable of:

* Monitoring sales and profit performance
* Evaluating discount strategies
* Identifying regional opportunities
* Supporting operational and management decisions
* Demonstrating business analytics and dashboarding skills

The portfolio consists of three business-focused dashboard projects built using the same Superstore retail dataset.

---

# 3. Included Projects

## Project 1 – Superstore Executive Dashboard

Executive-level dashboard for sales, profit, customer demand, and operational overview.

## Project 2 – Discount & Profitability Analysis Dashboard

Analytical dashboard focused on discount impact, loss-making products, and pricing strategy.

## Project 3 – Regional Performance & Market Analysis Dashboard

Regional intelligence dashboard for geographic sales and profitability analysis.
